Ethical Hacking

About

Ethical hacking, often referred to as "white-hat hacking," is the practice of identifying and addressing security vulnerabilities in systems to protect them from malicious attacks. Learning ethical hacking involves mastering the art of penetration testing, understanding network security, and gaining hands-on experience with tools like Metasploit, Wireshark, and Nmap. As you explore the field, you'll dive into topics like cryptography, social engineering, and web application security.
Price: 1,000,000

Cloud Computing

About

Cloud computing has revolutionized how businesses and individuals store, manage, and process data. Learning cloud computing means diving into a world where you can access powerful computing resources on demand, without the need for traditional hardware. You'll explore cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, mastering concepts such as virtualization, containerization, and serverless computing.
